Do you? Truly, deep down, looking past the amount of worldly success you have achieved? Maybe you really should be happy by anyone’s standards, but you are just... not. I dare you to face the shadows, leap, live life off the edge and ask yourself:

What if you lived awake and sexy, to your fullest expression of yourself, living life like you mean it? What if you were in total presence, joy and authenticity? What if you really lived your dreams? What if by doing all that you could actually help the planet?

That’s what my coaching is all about. Let’s defy gravity.

Coaching with me is for you if you resonate with any of these statements:

  • You feel unfulfilled in any aspect of your life
  • You are looking to gain clarity on any aspect of your life
  • You feel lost or stuck
  • You don’t feel you are really being and living you
  • You want to lift yourself higher
  • You are looking for a way to be in service to the planet


Coaching with me is by referral or through application.

My clients are high vibration individuals or those I affectionately call high vibration potentials and uniquees. This means that my clients tend to be one or more of the following:

  • already quite advanced/ high achievers/ do-it-themselves-ers
  • awake, seeking, or questioning (it feels like coming out of the closet a lot of the time, let’s face it!)
  • successful—as nebulous as that label is...
  • oddballs (I’m a proud member of this family myself.)
