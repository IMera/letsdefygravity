Why I do what I do...

I want for people to act and react in love. To wake up. To live their life like they mean it to their full expression of themselves. To care about themselves, and find themselves caring about all. To realise oneness. To discover their light and what it means to live from love. I want people to be love.

Because what is there more beautiful, more inspiring? Think about what that word means: Inspiring. Being filled with spirit, being in spirit. People lit up and present radiate magic that lets you sink to your knees in awe and weep. What feeling more glorious than that is there? What gift more glorious to receive? My passion is to give the gift of Light.

I believe that every individual fully owning their own Light invariably will be in service to those around them by doing so, and thus elevate the vibration of the planet.

Onwards and upwards on your starlight express,
